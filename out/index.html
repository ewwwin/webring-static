For webring participants:

  • You can view the full URL registered for your site by checking /list.
  • Link to the previous and next site in the ring with /prev and next. Both of these endpoints expect a query parameter `from` whose value should be the full URL of your site as shown in /list, including the scheme (http/https), optionally URI encoded. For example: https://webring.umbreon.online/prev?from=https://mysite.xyz.
  • If you need to change your registration or something, ping Erin on Discord or something.
